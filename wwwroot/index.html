Meet The Team

Donald G. Hooie, DDS

Dr. Hooie graduated with honors from the University of Tennessee College of Dentistry. Each year, Dr. Hooie continues his education with some of the most well-known clinicians in the country focusing on teeth-whitening, dentures, implants, crowns, dental bridges, tooth-colored fillings, non-surgical gum care, single-visit root canal therapy, laser dentistry, and adult orthodontics.

Dr. Hooie's memberships include the American Dental Association, the Tennessee Dental Association, the Crossville Dental Society, adn the Association of Military Surgeons. He is heighly-involved in helping those without access to qulity dental and is a volunteer dentist at Remote Area Medical, the Navajo Indian Reservation, the Kuna Indian Reservation, and in Panama.

When not in the offic, Dr. Hooie holds multiple positions at First united Methodist Church, including playin the bas with the praise band, Boy Scouts, and Crossville Lions Club, where he is a past president. He enjoys canoeing, kayaking, climbing/rappelling, and family time.
