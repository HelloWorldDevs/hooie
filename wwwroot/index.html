Meet The Team

Donald G. Hooie, DDS

“Since childhood, I have always wanted to help others and contribute to their improved health. I love the big smile when a patient sees their completed treatment for the first time. I want the best care for every patient and expect to give every patient comfortable treatment and the best-looking smile that functions well. “I am thorough and determined to complete each patient’s dental treatment comfortably and correctly. My goal is to preserve your natural teeth or replace them with proper esthetics and function.” In his free time, Dr. Hooie holds multiple positions at First United Methodist Church, including playing the bass with the Praise Band, Boy Scouts of America and Crossville Lions Club (past president). He enjoys canoeing, kayaking, climbing/rappelling and family time. Residency: General Practice, Portsmouth Naval Hospital, US Navy DDS: University of Tennessee College of Dentistry, with Honors BS: Chemistry/Physics/Biology, Middle Tennessee State University Dental Officer: US Navy, 1987 to 1993, Lieutenant Commander Licensed: State of Tennessee Member: American Dental Association, Tennessee Dental Association, Crossville Dental Society, Association of Military Surgeons of the US Certified: Laser Dentistry, Adult Orthodontics-Qualified Continuing Education: Over 8,000 hours including teeth whitening, dentures, restorations for implants, dental crowns, dental bridges, tooth-colored fillings, nonsurgical gum care, single-visit root canal therapy, laser dentistry, adult orthodontics and endodontics Past President: Crossville Lions Club, Melvin Jones Fellow Leader: Boy Scouts of America, Eagle Scout, Former Scout Master, Former Cub Master, Former District Chairman, Silver Beaver Recipient, Trainer, OA Vigil Honor Recipient Volunteer Dentist: Remote Area Medical; Navajo Indian Reservation; Kuna Indian Reservation, Panama Guest Speaker: Topics on oral health for local groups, schools and statewide organizations.
